Privacy policy

Last updated 7 July 2026

VibeFeed is a small social app for closed circles of friends. This page explains, simply and honestly, what information we process, why, and what rights you have.

Data controller

Deniz Filikci is the data controller for the processing of your information in VibeFeed.
Contact: official@vibefeed.dk

What data we process

  • Account: name, username, email, password (stored only as a hash), plus profile picture and bio if you add them.
  • Content: your posts, comments, images, short videos (6 seconds), poll votes, likes, circle memberships, friendships and activity.
  • Technical: your device's language choice, one notification token per device (for push notifications) and post reports.

Purposes and legal basis

We process your information to provide the service itself — your account, your feed, your circles and notifications. The legal basis is our agreement with you (GDPR article 6(1)(b)).

We only show personalized ads with your consent (GDPR article 6(1)(a)). You can withdraw your consent in the app at any time — ads then become non-personalized.

Data processors

  • Supabase — database and file storage. Data is stored in the EU (Stockholm region).
  • Vercel — hosting of the app itself.
  • Appodeal & Google AdMob — ads in the app. Your consent controls whether ads may be personalized.

Ads

The app shows ads via Appodeal and Google AdMob. Ads cannot be opted out of, but personalization can: without consent, only non-personalized ads are shown. You choose the first time you open the app, and you can always change your choice under Edit profile → Privacy.

Storage and deletion

We keep your information until you delete it yourself — individual posts/comments can be deleted at any time. If you choose Delete account in the app, your profile, posts, comments, images, videos and all other information are removed permanently.

Your rights

You have the right to access your information, the right to have incorrect information corrected, and the right to deletion. Write to official@vibefeed.dk and we'll help.

You can also complain to the Danish Data Protection Agency (Datatilsynet): www.datatilsynet.dk

Cookies and local storage

We don't use tracking cookies. The app only stores these things locally on your device:

  • your language choice (vf_lang)
  • your ad consent choice (vf_consent)
  • your login session (so you don't have to log in every time)
  • the device's notification token (vf_device_secret)
  • when you last opened your circles (vf_feed_seen)

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: official@vibefeed.dk
